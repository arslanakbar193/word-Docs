Addendum to Tenancy Contract
(Non – Negotiable)

Tenant to adhere below stated terms:
■ Tenant will be liable for a penalty of AED 500 for every dishonoured cheque payable to landlord.
■ Tenant agrees to pay a fee of AED 100 per day in case of request to hold the cheque and a fee of AED 100 per replacement of cheque.
■ 60 days prior notice to be provided in case of non-renewal of contract.
■ A prior notice of 60 days to be given in case of early termination and rent to be settled for notice period (60 days) in advance.
■ Subleasing, sharing, and occupancy by bachelors are strictly prohibited. If any violation of these terms is discovered, the contract will be terminated with a 7days notice.
■ Tenant agrees to sign the Tenancy Contract with submission of rental cheques along with any other related fee within the Contract expiry. Tenant agrees to pay AED 100 per day after the above timeline as delay penalty.
