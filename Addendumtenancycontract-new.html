ADDENDUM TO TENANCY CONTRACT

This Addendum shall form part of the Tenancy Contract commencingand must be read in conjunction with the Contract between the “Landlord” and the “Tenant”The “premises”shall mean the property including gardens, common communal entrances and facilities. “Fixtures, Furnishings & Fittings”shall mean all that is now in or on the premises.

The tenant and landlord agree as follows:

1.
Security Deposit
1.1
The Tenant shall pay AED /- as a rent for limited one-year tenancy contract AED /- as security deposit. This will be refunded to the tenant when vacating the premises except to the extent that damages (fair wear and tear excluded) have been caused to the premises and copy of receipts of all paid final bills being given to the landlord. This contract is valid up to the end of specified period after which date it is consider null and void.
2.
Use of Premises
2.1
It is understood that the tenant will use the premises for residential purpose only and the tenant shall not use the premises to carry out any profession, trade or business whatsoever.
2.2
The tenant is not allowed to transfer, assign, sublet, or lease the property to any third party
2.3
The tenant shall not make any addition or alteration to the premises without the written consent of the landlord
2.4
The erection of any board or notice visible from outside of the property advertising any profession, trade, business, goods or service is prohibited.
2.5
The tenant shall not do or permit any act that may cause injury, damage, nuisance or annoyance to a neighboring property or person and shall respect any community by laws
2.6
The premises shall not be used for any illegal or immoral purpose in contravention of any emirate regulation or by law.
2.7
The keeping of any pet on the premises is strictly prohibited by the landlord.
2.8
The tenant shall not glue carpet or wooden grippers to the floor surfaces.
3.
Terms of Payment
3.1
The tenant shall pay the rent for one year in advance in cheques.
3.2
The tenant understands that non-clearance of a cheque is unlawful and, in such event, the tenant will have to pay AED 1,000/- as a penalty. Landlord can report to the local authorities and evict the tenant in case of nonpayment.
4.
Interior Furnishing
4.1
The tenant shall not remove any item of fixtures, and fittings during and after the tenancy period.
4.2
Insuring the contents of the property is the responsibility of the tenant
5.
Re-installment/Reconstruction
5.1
No repairs/ alterations (inside/outside) effecting the structure and beauty of the building / premises to be carried out by the tenant without the permission of the landlord.
5.2
The tenant agrees to make good repair or cover replacement cost (at the landlord’s decision) any item damaged by the tenant, his family or guest.
6.
Maintenance and Utilities
6.1
The tenant will keep the interior of the premises and fixtures and fittings (Including paint and decorations) in good condition during tenancy
6.2
The Tenant shall be responsible for all utility consumption charges during the tenancy, including DEWA, AC(Chiller), municipality taxes & government charges, telephone, television and internet charges.
6.3
Major maintenance and community service charges are the responsibility of the landlord. Landlord agrees to provide a copy of paid service charges.
6.4
The Tenant shall be responsible for the maintenance and associated charges for the lawns, shrubs and trees if present with the property
6.5
The tenant shall be responsible for minor maintenance under AED 1000/-.
6.6
The landlord shall be responsible for major maintenance above AED 1000/-.
6.7
The tenant shall permit the landlord or his representative access to the premises (upon reasonable notice) for the purpose of major maintenance or for inspecting the premises.
6.8
At the time of vacating the premises, the tenant will pay up and produce the receipts for the final DEWA, AC (Chiller) and utility bills.
6.9
The Tenant is solely responsible for obtaining insurance coverage for their personal belongings, including furniture and appliances, throughout the duration of their tenancy. The Landlord bears no responsibility in the event of damage resulting from any accidents occurring during the tenancy period.
6.10
The tenant undertakes to immediately notify the landlord or his representative in the event of flood or other incident involving the premises or own discovery of any damage or fault in the premises.
7.
End of Tenancy and Renewal
7.1
This Tenancy Contract may be renewed after one year, subject to the tenant giving the Landlord at least 90 days’ notice, in writing, prior to the expiry date and the landlord accepting, in writing. Failure to make payment or give written notification of renewal will result in repossession of the premises within 24 hours of the contract expiry date
7.2
If the tenant does not wish to extend the tenancy, he agrees to submit in advance 90 days written notice to the landlord before the expiry of tenancy contract. The tenant also agrees to give access in the last 2 weeks of the tenancy to show prospective new tenants, being given at least one-day notice for viewings.
7.3
The Tenant shall clean the premises and return it as it was handed over to the tenant at the time of occupying it.
7.4
Annual rent for the Renewal is subject to change depending on the RERA calculator.
7.5
If any interior painting alterations have been made, the tenant agrees to re-paint the apartment prior to vacating the premises or pay the landlord for the cost of such re-painting to original condition.
7.6
Incase tenant will break this tenancy contract he has to pay 2-months penalty.
7.7
In the event of the Landlord and Tenant are extending the tenancy for further one year a rent review shall take place according to the current RERA, municipal guidelines and RERA rental calculators. Union Square House Real Estate Brokers will charge AED 1,000 + VAT for agency renewal fee.
8.
Sale of Property
8.1
The Tenant agrees to allow viewing of the premise to prospective buyers in the event landlord wishes to sell subject to a notice of 48 hours and not more than 2 viewings a week.
8.2
In the event of such sale happening the Landlord will introduce the Tenant to the new purchaser and transfer all the documents under new purchaser’s name.
8.3
If the Tenant purchases the property from Landlord, Union Square House Real Estate Brokers will be entitled to a fee 2% of the sale value as introduction fees from the Tenant.
