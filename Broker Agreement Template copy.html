OBLIGATIONS AND RESPONSIBILITIES OF THE SUB AGENT:
I.
The Sub Agent shall be responsible to offer the Assigned Units for a consideration amount as per the pricing strategy of Principal.
II.
The Sub Agent is prohibited from using portal advertising, for generating sales leads, unless granted written authorization/No Objection letter (NOC) by the Principal. Portal advertising for generating rental leads for Assigned Units in the Project is strictly prohibited.
III.
The role of the Sub Agent shall be to assist the Principal and the Prospective Buyer with the sale transaction and ensure that all the rules and regulations of RAK Land Department/RAK Central/ Government and the rules specifically mentioned in the Law no.
“Regulating the Real Estate Brokers in the Emirates of UAE” along with the related Regulation(s), circular(s) issued from time to time are complied with.
IV.
The Sub Agent cannot do any kind of Marketing or any branding activity without prior written approval of the Developer.
V.
The Sub Agent on behalf of the principal shall not collect any payment(s) and or cheque(s) in his favor from the Prospective Buyer. .
